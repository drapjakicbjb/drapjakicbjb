A Legacy of Learning & Leadership

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Inter College, Belwariya Jungle, Basti, Uttar Pradesh was established with the inspired belief that quality education transforms lives. Named after India's beloved "Missile Man" and former President, our school embodies his values of discipline, innovation, and lifelong learning.

We provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where students from Primary to Higher Secondary level receive a well-rounded education that blends strong academics with co-curricular achievement.

Our Mission

To empower every student with knowledge, values, and skills to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow's India.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted educational institution in Basti, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and innovation.

Academics

Our Academic Programmes

Comprehensive education from primary to higher secondary, designed to ignite curiosity and build strong foundations.

Primary School

Classes Pre K.G.–V. A nurturing foundation using activity-based, playful learning approaches to build literacy, numeracy, and curiosity.

Middle School

Classes VI–VIII. Bridging foundational learning with structured subjects across languages, science, mathematics and social sciences.

Secondary School

Classes IX–X (UP Board). Rigorous curriculum with lab-based science, comprehensive mathematics and language proficiency.

Higher School

Classes XI–XII (UP Board). Streams: Science (PCM / PCB) and Arts. Expert guidance for board exams and competitive entrances.

Co-curricular Activities

Art, debate, sports and cultural programmes nurture talent beyond textbooks for all-round student development.

Admissions

Join Our School Family

We welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and make a difference. Admissions are open for 2026–27 academic year.

1

Collect Application Form

Visit the school office or download the form from our website. Fill in all the required details carefully.

2

Submit Required Documents

Submit the filled form along with transfer certificate, previous marksheets, Aadhaar card, and two passport-size photos.

3

Entrance Assessment

Students seeking admission to Class III or above will appear for a simple written test in Hindi, English, Mathematics etc.

4

Fee Payment & Enrolment

Upon selection, pay the admission fee at the office to confirm enrolment and receive your admission kit.

5

Welcome to the School!

Collect your uniform, ID card and books, and begin your journey at our school.

🎓 Admissions Open 2026–27

Enrolment is open for classes Nursery to Class XII. We encourage early application to secure your child's place in our school.
